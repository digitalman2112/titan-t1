## Setup

1. Attach the <span class="ref-accessory">safety spotters</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S2</span> onto the <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span> at the desired height. Make sure that both safety bars are set at the proper height to limit the lowest desired safe range of travel of the EZ curl bar and weights.
2. Carefully load the desired weight on the EZ curl bar. Do not exceed your known performance capabilities.
3. Ensure that weights are evenly distributed on each side of the EZ curl bar. Always use locking collars to secure the weight plates to the bar.

## Standing Bar Curl

1. Stand facing the machine and firmly grasp the EZ curl bar.
2. Lift and lower the EZ curl bar in a slow, controlled manner.

## After Completing

1. Return the EZ curl bar to rest on the <span class="ref-accessory">safety spotters</span>.
2. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">safety spotters</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S2</span> from the hooks.

## Precautions

- Ensure that weights are evenly distributed on each side of the EZ curl bar.
- Always use locking collars to secure the weight plates to the bar.
