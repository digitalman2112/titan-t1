## Setup

1. Raise the seat to the highest position and lock into place.
2. Connect the <span class="ref-accessory">preacher curl attachment</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S7</span> into hole **27** located on the seat.
3. Carefully load the desired weight on the EZ curl bar. Do not exceed your known performance capabilities.
4. Center the EZ curl bar on the rubber pads below the <span class="ref-accessory">preacher curl pad</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S7</span>.
5. Adjust the seat height to the desired position by pulling up on the back support of the bench. After the bench is properly positioned, ensure that it is locked into place.

## Preacher Curl

1. Sit on the bench facing the machine and firmly grasp the EZ curl bar.
2. In a slow, controlled manner, lift the EZ curl bar toward the body.
3. Pause briefly in the fully contracted position and return slowly to the starting position.

## After Completing

1. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">preacher curl attachment pad</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S7</span>.
2. Remove the EZ curl bar.

## Precautions

- Be sure that weights are evenly distributed on each side of the EZ curl bar.
- Always use locking collars to secure the weight plates to the bar.
- After the bench is properly positioned, ensure that it is locked into place.
