## Setup

1. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span> into hole **3-L** and handle <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> into hole **3-R**. Lift the smith carriage and lock onto hooks <span class="ref-hook">H-2</span> or <span class="ref-hook">H-3</span>.
2. Adjust the backrest to the desired position using the pull pin located behind the seat.
3. Adjust the seat height to the desired position using the pull pin located underneath the seat.
4. Adjust the bench to position **21** (adjust to preference) and be sure that it is locked into place.
5. If the weight of the smith carriage without added weight is too heavy, insert <span class="ref-cable">counter-balance cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> into hole **18** and add the desired counter weight.
6. Be sure that the weights are evenly distributed on each side of the machine.
7. Enter the machine carefully. Assume a comfortable reclining position with your back resting firmly against the back of the seat and your feet on the provided footrest. Place feet squarely on the ground approximately shoulder-width apart.

## Shoulder Press

1. In a slow and controlled manner, unlock both <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> and lift weight by pushing the <span class="ref-accessory">handles</span> upwards.
2. Pause briefly in the near fully extended position, and return to the starting position.
3. When finished, lock both <span class="ref-accessory">handles</span> securely onto the <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span>.

## After Completing

1. If a counter-balance was used, remove any weight from the weight post, then remove <span class="ref-cable">cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> from hole **18** and return it to its storage hole.
2. Remove any weight from the weight posts.
3. Unlock the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> and carefully lower the smith carriage so it rests on the springs at the base of the machine.

## Precautions

- Exercise extreme caution when loading and unloading the weight plates.
- Be sure that the weights are evenly distributed on each side of the machine.
- After the bench is properly positioned, ensure that it is locked into place.
- Always ensure **both** <span class="ref-accessory">handles</span> are securely locked before starting and when finishing. Never secure the smith carriage by locking only one side.
