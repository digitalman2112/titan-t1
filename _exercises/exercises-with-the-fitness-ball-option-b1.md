## Setup

1. Slide the bench completely forward.
2. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">footplate</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S3-L</span> into hole **22-R** and <span class="ref-accessory">footplate</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S3-R</span> into hole **22-L**.
3. Add weight (approximately 45 lbs.) to the leg curl/extension weight post.
4. Insert the <span class="ref-accessory">roller pad</span> <span class="ref-accessory">B-2</span> into hole **29**, **30**, or **31** depending on your preference.
5. Anchor the feet under the <span class="ref-accessory">roller pad</span> <span class="ref-accessory">B-2</span> and place both feet firmly onto the <span class="ref-accessory">footplates</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S3-L</span> and <span class="ref-accessory">S3-R</span>.

## Exercises with the Fitness Ball

1. Position the fitness ball behind your lower back or as needed for the chosen exercise.
2. Begin the exercise (sit-ups, back extensions, stretching, etc.) in a slow, controlled manner.
3. Pause briefly in the fully contracted position and return to the starting position.

## Precautions

- Ensure the roller pad and footplates are securely inserted before use.
- Do not exceed your known performance capabilities.
