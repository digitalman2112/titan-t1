## Setup

1. Insert the locking <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span> into hole **4-L**. Before inserting the opposite handle, slide one end of the <span class="ref-accessory">tube</span> <span class="ref-accessory">(option N)</span> over the grip of <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span>.
2. Then put the locking <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> into hole **4-R**. Now slide the <span class="ref-accessory">tube</span> over the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> so that the tube is centered between the two handles and lock into place by twisting the knob on each side of the tube.
3. Lift the smith carriage and lock onto <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span> <span class="ref-hook">H-2</span> or <span class="ref-hook">H-3</span>.
4. **CAREFULLY** load the desired weight. **DO NOT EXCEED YOUR KNOWN PERFORMANCE CAPABILITIES!** Be sure that the weights are evenly distributed on each side of the machine.
5. Adjust the backrest to the desired position using the pull pin located behind the seat.
6. Adjust the seat height to the desired position using the pull pin located underneath the seat.
7. Adjust the bench to position **16** (adjust to preference) and be sure that it is locked into place. **Note:** Test the positioning of the bench to assure that the smith carriage crossbar will not come in contact with the head or the end of the backrest. This is especially important when the bench is inclined. Adjust the bench, if necessary, and lock into place.

## Close-Grip Shallow Incline Press

1. Enter the machine carefully. Assume a comfortable reclining position with your back resting firmly against the back of the seat and your feet on the provided footrest.
2. In a slow and controlled manner, unlock the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> and lift weight by pushing the exercise handles (<span class="ref-accessory">tube</span>) upwards. Pause briefly in the near fully extended position, and return to the starting position.
3. If the weight of the smith carriage without added weight is too heavy, insert <span class="ref-cable">counter-balance cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> into hole **18** for counter-balance and then add the desired counter weight.
4. Be sure that both handles of the smith machine are securely locked into place when completing the exercise. Do not attempt to secure the smith machine by locking only one side.
5. Instructions can also be found on the video CD that was included with your machine.

## Exercise Precautions

- After the bench is properly positioned, ensure that it is locked into place.
- Exercise extreme caution when loading and unloading the weight plates.

## After Completing

1. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">tube</span> from the handles.
2. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> from the smith machine.
3. If the counter-balance was used, remove any weight from the weight post and then remove <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> cable from hole **18** and return to the storage hole.
