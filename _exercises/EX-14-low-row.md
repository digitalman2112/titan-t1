## Setup

1. Move the bench completely forward to position **1** and incline the backrest to the highest position.
2. Attach <span class="ref-cable">lat pulley cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C3</span> to the attachment pin located in the center of the smith carriage crossbar.
3. Using a clip, attach <span class="ref-cable">extension cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C4</span> to the end of <span class="ref-cable">C3</span> hanging overhead at the forefront of the machine.
4. Remove any other exercise bar from the safety clip and attach the low row handles to the other end of <span class="ref-cable">C4</span>.
5. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">footplate</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S3-L</span> into hole **21-R** and footplate <span class="ref-accessory">S3-R</span> into hole **21-L** on the lat pulley support bar.
6. Add the desired weight and be sure that the weights are evenly distributed on each side of the machine.
7. If the weight of the smith carriage without added weight is too heavy, insert <span class="ref-cable">counter-balance cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> into hole **18** and add the desired counter weight.

## Low Row

1. Sit upright on the floor and place feet squarely on the <span class="ref-accessory">footplates</span>. Keep knees slightly bent (flexed).
2. Firmly grasp the low row handles with both hands.
3. In a slow and controlled manner, pull the low row handles inward toward the lower chest area.
4. Pause briefly in the fully contracted position and return to the starting position.
5. Do not lean forward or backward, or use the lower back.

## After Completing

1. If a counter-balance was used, remove any weight from the weight post, then remove <span class="ref-cable">cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> from hole **18** and return it to its storage hole.
2. Unclip and remove <span class="ref-cable">C4</span> from <span class="ref-cable">C3</span>.
3. Remove <span class="ref-cable">C3</span> from the locking pin and hook onto the storage pin for C3.
4. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">footplates</span>.
