## Setup

1. Attach <span class="ref-cable">counter-balance cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> into hole **18** located at the center of the smith carriage crossbar.
2. Slide the bench forward to position **1**.
3. Attach <span class="ref-accessory">pull-up handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S10-R</span> into hole **24-L** and handle <span class="ref-accessory">S10-L</span> into hole **24-R**. The handles can be placed in several orientations to preference.
4. Place <span class="ref-accessory">dip bar</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S6-R</span> into hole **12-R** and <span class="ref-accessory">S6-L</span> into hole **12-L**. Center the <span class="ref-accessory">preacher curl pad</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S7</span> so that it rests on <span class="ref-accessory">S6-L</span> and <span class="ref-accessory">S6-R</span>.
5. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> into hole **4-L** and handle <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span> into hole **4-R**.
6. Carefully begin loading weight plates onto the counter-balance weight posts. Be careful — the weight will shift once it exceeds the smith carriage weight plus the weight of the attachments.
7. Push down on the <span class="ref-accessory">support pad</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S7</span> so that it moves downwards enough to lock the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> and <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span> underneath the <span class="ref-hook">H-9</span> hooks.

## Assisted Pull Ups

1. Grab the <span class="ref-accessory">pull-up handles</span> with one hand and press down on the <span class="ref-accessory">support pad</span> with the opposite knee. Unlock the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span> and <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> with your free hand.
2. Firmly grab the <span class="ref-accessory">pull-up handles</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S10-R</span> and <span class="ref-accessory">S10-L</span> with both hands and bring the other knee up onto the <span class="ref-accessory">support pad</span> and begin pull-ups.
3. When finished, lock the <span class="ref-accessory">handles</span> underneath hooks <span class="ref-hook">H-9</span>-L and <span class="ref-hook">H-9</span>-R and exit the support platform.

## After Completing

1. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">support pad</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S7</span>.
2. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">dip bar handles</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S6-R</span> and <span class="ref-accessory">S6-L</span>.
3. Carefully remove the weight from the counter-balance weight post. When the weight begins to shift, the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> will rest on the <span class="ref-hook">H-9</span> hooks.
4. Unlock the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> and lower the smith carriage so it rests on the springs at the base of the machine.
5. Remove <span class="ref-cable">cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> from hole **18** and return to storage for C2.

## Precautions

- Be sure that <span class="ref-accessory">pull-up handles</span> are securely inserted into the attachment slots and will not slide out.
- Check that all cables and attachments are properly connected.
