## Setup

1. Slide the bench forward to position **1** and raise the backrest to the highest position.
2. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> into hole **10-L** and <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span> into hole **10-R**. Lift the smith carriage by the handles and lock onto <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span> <span class="ref-hook">H-13</span>.
3. Insert the <span class="ref-accessory">back rest pad</span> <span class="ref-accessory">C-1</span> into hole **18** on the back side of the smith carriage.
4. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">forearm pad</span> <span class="ref-accessory">C2-R</span> into hole **16-R** and <span class="ref-accessory">forearm pad</span> <span class="ref-accessory">C2-L</span> into hole **16-L**. If the pads are too narrow, widen by inserting one or both forearm pads into hole **15**.

## Ab Station

1. With the back resting comfortably against the <span class="ref-accessory">back pad</span> <span class="ref-accessory">C-1</span>, firmly grasp the vertical handles.
2. Let body weight rest on the <span class="ref-accessory">forearm pads</span>.
3. In a slow, controlled manner, raise legs. Pause briefly in the fully contracted position and return to the starting position.
4. Upon completion of exercise, gently lower body off of the shoulder pads.

## Exercise Precautions

- Put the locking <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> on the <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span> at a height that positions the backrest just above the waist. Do not rest the smith carriage on the springs at the bottom. Always lock the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> on the <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span>.
- Be sure that the <span class="ref-accessory">forearm pads</span> and <span class="ref-accessory">back pad</span> are securely inserted into the attachment slots and will not slide out.
