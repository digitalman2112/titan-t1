## Setup

1. Put the locking <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> into hole **7-R** and <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span> into hole **7-L**. Lift the smith carriage by the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> and lock onto <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span> <span class="ref-hook">H-5</span>.
2. Adjust the backrest to the desired position using the pull pin underneath and lock into place.
3. Insert the <span class="ref-accessory">decline attachment</span> <span class="ref-accessory">D-1</span> into hole **26** located at the top of the backrest.
4. Adjust the bench to position **15** (adjust to preference) and lock into place.
5. Be sure that the <span class="ref-accessory">decline attachment</span> is inserted securely.

## Decline Bench with Smith Machine

1. Enter the machine carefully, placing feet beneath the lower rollers with the back of the knees bent across the upper rollers. Lay down in the declined position.
2. If the weight of the smith carriage without added weight is too heavy, insert <span class="ref-cable">counter-balance cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> into hole **18** for counter-balance and then add the desired counter weight.
3. In a slow and controlled manner, lift weight by the handles and unlock, extend arms upwards, pause briefly with the arms near fully extended position, and return to the starting position.
4. Be sure that both handles of the smith machine are securely locked into place when completing the exercise. Do not attempt to secure the smith machine by locking only one side.
5. Exit the machine carefully.
6. Instructions can also be found on the video CD that was included with your machine.

## Exercise Precautions

- Be sure that the <span class="ref-accessory">decline attachment</span> is inserted securely.

## After Completing

1. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">decline attachment</span> <span class="ref-accessory">D-1</span>.
2. If the counter-balance was used, remove any weight from the weight post and then remove <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> cable from hole **18** and return to the storage hole.
3. Remove any weight from the weight post and lower the smith carriage to rest on the springs.
