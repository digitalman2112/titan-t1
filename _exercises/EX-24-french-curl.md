## Setup

1. Adjust the seat to the desired position using the pull pin located behind the seat. After the bench is properly positioned, ensure that it is locked into place.
2. Attach the <span class="ref-accessory">safety spotters</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S2</span> onto hooks <span class="ref-hook">H-5</span> or <span class="ref-hook">H-6</span>, according to preference.
3. Load the desired weight on the EZ curl bar and rest it on the <span class="ref-accessory">safety spotters</span>.

## French Curl

1. Sit on the bench facing the machine. Grab the EZ curl bar firmly and assume a reclining position with your back resting firmly against the backrest and your feet planted firmly on the ground.
2. Lift and lower the EZ curl bar in a smooth and controlled manner. Keeping the elbows in a vertical position, raise and lower the bar to a point just above the head.
3. Carefully place the EZ curl bar back on the <span class="ref-accessory">safety spotters</span> after completing the exercise.

## After Completing

1. Re-rack the EZ curl bar onto the <span class="ref-accessory">safety spotters</span>.
2. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">safety spotters</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S2</span> from the hooks.

## Precautions

- Ensure that weights are evenly distributed on each side of the EZ curl bar.
- After the bench is properly positioned, ensure that it is locked into place.
