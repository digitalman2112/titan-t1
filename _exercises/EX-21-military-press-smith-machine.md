## Setup

1. Adjust the bench to position **1** (adjust to preference) and lock into place.
2. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> into hole **5-R** and handle <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span> into hole **5-L**. Lift the smith carriage and lock onto hooks <span class="ref-hook">H-2</span> or <span class="ref-hook">H-3</span>.
3. If the weight is too much, insert <span class="ref-cable">counter-balance cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> into hole **18** and add weight to the counter-balance weight post.
4. Add the desired weight, distributed evenly on both sides.

## Military Press

1. In a slow and controlled manner, push upward on the <span class="ref-accessory">smith machine handles</span>, unlock, and lift the weight overhead.
2. Pause briefly in the near fully extended position and then return to the starting position.
3. Do not arch your back while performing the exercise.
4. When finished, lock both <span class="ref-accessory">handles</span> securely onto the <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span>.

## After Completing

1. Remove any weight from the counter-balance weight post.
2. Carefully remove <span class="ref-cable">cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> from hole **18** and insert into storage for C2.
3. Unlock the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> and lower the smith carriage so it rests on the springs at the base of the machine.

## Precautions

- Always ensure **both** <span class="ref-accessory">handles</span> are securely locked before starting and when finishing. Never secure the smith carriage by locking only one side.
- After the bench is properly positioned, ensure that it is locked into place.
- Do not arch your back while performing the exercise.
