## Setup

1. Attach <span class="ref-cable">counter-balance cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> into hole **18** for counter balance. Add enough weight to lighten the smith carriage.
2. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> into hole **4-R** and <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span> into hole **4-L**. Lift the smith carriage to <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span> <span class="ref-hook">H-1</span>.
3. Place the Olympic bar onto <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span> <span class="ref-hook">H-13</span>.
4. Move the bench completely forward to position **1** and lock into place.
5. Incline the backrest to approximately 45° and lock the pull pin located underneath the backrest.
6. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">seat attachment</span> <span class="ref-accessory">B-1</span> into hole **26** located on the top of the backrest. Be sure it is securely attached.
7. Lower the seat (B-1) by unlocking the pin of the backrest and resting the seat on the Olympic bar.
8. Insert the <span class="ref-accessory">leg extension footpad</span> <span class="ref-accessory">B-2</span> into hole **30** of the leg extension/leg curl attachment. The footpad can be adjusted to a different hole (**29** or **31**) if it is uncomfortable while performing the exercise.
9. Adjust the leg curl/extension arm by unlocking the lever and rotating the arm to the desired position (9–10 o'clock position for leg curl; 6 o'clock position for leg extension). Lock the lever into place by pulling back on the handle.
10. Add the desired weight to the weight post of the leg curl/extension attachment.

## Leg Curl / Extension

1. Sit on the bench and position feet under the <span class="ref-accessory">leg extension footpad</span> <span class="ref-accessory">B-2</span>.
2. In a slow, controlled manner, perform the desired exercise (leg curl or leg extension).
3. Pause briefly in the fully contracted position and return slowly to the starting position.
4. Instructions for leg curl or leg extension can also be found on the video CD that was included with your Titan.

## Prior to Using This Exercise

- Be sure that the smith carriage is lifted up and out of the way.
- Be sure that the attachments are locked into place.

## After Completing

1. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">leg extension footpad</span> <span class="ref-accessory">B-2</span>.
2. Raise the backrest off of the Olympic bar and lock into place with the pull pin behind the backrest.
3. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">seat attachment</span> <span class="ref-accessory">B-1</span>.
4. Remove the Olympic bar from <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span> <span class="ref-hook">H-13</span>.
5. Unlock the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S-1</span> and lower the smith carriage to rest on the springs.
6. Remove the weight from the counter balance post.
7. Remove <span class="ref-cable">cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> from hole **18** and place pin into storage for C-2.

## Precautions

- Be sure that the smith carriage is lifted up and out of the way before performing this exercise.
- Be sure that all attachments are locked into place before use.
