## Setup

1. Slide the bench completely forward to position **1** and incline the backrest to the high position.
2. Attach the <span class="ref-accessory">safety spotters</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S2</span> onto the hooks at the desired height. Make sure that both safety bars are set at the proper height to limit the lowest desired safe range of travel of the olympic bar and weights.
3. Place the olympic bar onto the proper hooks to begin your exercise.
4. Ensure that the bar is centered on the apparatus and evenly distribute the desired weights on each side of the bar.
5. Maintain a stable base of support with your feet planted securely on the floor.

## Exercise

1. In a slow and controlled manner, perform the exercise.
2. Pause briefly at the end of the range of motion and return to the starting position.
3. When racking the bar, make sure that the bar is securely retained by the barbell supports before releasing your pressure and grip on the bar.

## After Completing

1. Re-rack the olympic bar securely onto the original hooks.
2. Remove all weight from the bar.
3. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">safety spotters</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S2</span> from the hooks and store them.

## Precautions

- Make sure that both <span class="ref-accessory">safety spotters</span> are set at the proper height to limit the lowest desired safe range of travel of the olympic bar and weights.
- Ensure that the bar is centered on the apparatus and that weights are evenly distributed on each side of the bar.
- Perform the exercise in a slow, controlled manner. Avoid jerky movements.
