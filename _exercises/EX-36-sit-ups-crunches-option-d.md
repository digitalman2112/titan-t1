## Setup

1. Slide the bench to position **12** (adjust to preference) and lock into place.
2. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span> into hole **11-R** and <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> into hole **11-L** on the back side of the smith carriage.
3. Using the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span>, lift the smith carriage and lock onto <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span> <span class="ref-hook">H-10</span>.
4. Adjust the backrest to the desired inclined position using the pull pin located behind the back of the seat. **NOTE:** Height adjustment of the board depends upon the user's strength and ability. It is important that beginners do not overdo it! Start with the board set in the lowest hole and work your way up.
5. Insert the <span class="ref-accessory">decline attachment</span> <span class="ref-accessory">D-1</span> into hole **26** located at the top of the backrest.

## Sit Ups / Crunches

1. Enter the machine carefully, placing feet beneath the lower rollers with the back of the knees bent across the upper rollers. Lay down in the declined position.
2. In a slow and controlled manner, curl the chin toward the knees.
3. Exhale at completion (top) of the sit up, forcefully contracting the abdominal muscles. Inhale on the way down to the start position.
4. Repeat, bending as far forward as possible.
5. Exit the machine carefully.
6. Instructions can also be found on the video CD that was included with your machine.

## Exercise Precautions

- After the bench is properly positioned, ensure that it is locked into place.
- Be sure that the <span class="ref-accessory">decline attachment</span> is inserted securely.

## After Completing

1. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">decline attachment</span> <span class="ref-accessory">D-1</span>.
2. Lower the backrest to the flat position.
3. Unlock the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> and lower the smith carriage to rest on the bottom springs.
