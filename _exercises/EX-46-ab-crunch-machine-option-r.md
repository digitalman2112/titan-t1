## Setup

1. Put the locking <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> into hole **1-L** on the back side of the smith carriage. Insert the <span class="ref-accessory">tube</span> <span class="ref-accessory">(option N)</span> onto the grip of the <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> handle.
2. Next, insert the locking <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span> into hole **1-R**. Center the <span class="ref-accessory">tube</span> between the handles and lock into place by twisting the knobs on each end of the tube.
3. Adjust the bench to position **8** (adjust to preference) and lock into place.
4. Insert the <span class="ref-accessory">ABS attachment R-1</span> into hole **27** located on the seat.

## Ab Crunch Machine

1. Enter the machine carefully, placing heels on the <span class="ref-accessory">tube</span> <span class="ref-accessory">(option N)</span> and resting the front of the feet on the backside of the smith carriage crossbar.
2. Add the desired weight to the weight post of the <span class="ref-accessory">R-1 attachment</span>.
3. Place head on the headrest and grab the grips of the <span class="ref-accessory">ABS attachment R-1</span>.
4. In a slow and controlled manner, curl chin toward knees.
5. Exhale at completion (top) of the sit up, forcefully contracting the abdominal muscles. Inhale on the way down to start position.
6. Repeat exercise. **Note:** Raising too high during the exercise may cause the pin from the seat to lock. If this occurs, simply lift the seat slightly to unlock the bench and lower it to the start position. Continue the exercise.
7. After finishing the exercise, exit the machine carefully.
8. Instructions can also be found on the video CD that was included with your machine.

## Exercise Precautions

- Be sure that the <span class="ref-accessory">ABS attachment</span> is inserted securely.
- Perform the exercise in a slow and controlled manner. Fast, jerky motions may cause the weight to discharge from the weight post causing injury.

## After Completing

1. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">tube</span> from the handles.
2. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> from the smith machine.
3. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">R-1 attachment</span> from the seat.
