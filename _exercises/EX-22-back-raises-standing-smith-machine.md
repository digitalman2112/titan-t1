## Setup

1. Move the bench completely forward to position **1** and incline the backrest to the highest position.
2. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> into hole **8-L** and handle <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span> into hole **8-R** on the back side of the smith carriage. The grips should face inward. Lock into place.
3. Lift the smith carriage onto hooks <span class="ref-hook">H-11</span>.
4. If the weight of the smith carriage without added weight is too heavy, insert <span class="ref-cable">counter-balance cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> into hole **18** for counter-balance and add the desired counter weight.
5. Add the desired weight, distributed evenly on both sides.

## Back Raises

1. Stand facing away from the machine, firmly grasp the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> and unlock each side.
2. Lift the weight with the shoulders and arms in an upward direction.
3. Pause briefly and return to the starting position.
4. Maintain a stable base of support with your feet on the lower frame brace while performing the exercise.
5. When finished, lock both <span class="ref-accessory">handles</span> securely onto the <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span>.

## After Completing

1. If a counter-balance was used, remove any weight from the weight post, then remove <span class="ref-cable">cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> from hole **18** and return it to its storage hole.
2. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span>.

## Precautions

- Check to ensure that all cables and attachments are properly attached and inserted.
- Always ensure **both** <span class="ref-accessory">handles</span> are securely locked before starting and when finishing. Never secure the smith carriage by locking only one side.
