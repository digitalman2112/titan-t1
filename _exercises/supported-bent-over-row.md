## Setup

1. Slide the bench to position **22** (adjust to preference) and lock into place.
2. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span> into hole **12-L** and handle <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> into hole **12-R** on the back side of the smith carriage. The grips should face inward.
3. Using the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span>, lift the smith carriage and lock onto hooks <span class="ref-hook">H-13</span>.
4. Adjust the backrest (it will serve as the chest pad for this exercise) up to approximately a 45° angle and lock into place using the pull pin located behind the backrest.
5. Test the positioning of the bench to ensure that the smith carriage crossbar will not come in contact with the head or back during the exercise. Adjust the bench accordingly and lock into place.
6. If the weight of the smith carriage without added weight is too heavy, insert <span class="ref-cable">counter-balance cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> into hole **18** and add the desired counter weight.
7. Add the desired weight, distributed evenly on both sides.

## Bent Over Row

1. Lean slightly forward and place your chest directly against the chest pad (backrest).
2. Grasp the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> firmly and twist to unlock.
3. In a slow, controlled manner, pull the <span class="ref-accessory">handles</span> toward the body in a rowing fashion.
4. Pause briefly in the fully flexed arm position and then return to the starting position.
5. Upon completion, lower the weight by slowly extending arms to the original starting position and lock both <span class="ref-accessory">handles</span> onto the <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span>.

## After Completing

1. Unlock the bench and slide it forward.
2. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span> and <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> from the smith carriage.
3. If a counter-balance was used, remove any weight from the weight post, then remove <span class="ref-cable">cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> from hole **18** and return it to its storage hole.

## Precautions

- After the bench is properly positioned, ensure that it is locked into place.
- Enter and exit the machine very carefully.
- Check that all cables and attachments are properly connected.
- Securely lock the bench in a position that will not allow the smith carriage bar to come near the head or back while performing the exercise.
- **EXIT MACHINE CAREFULLY.**
