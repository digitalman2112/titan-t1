## Setup

1. Attach <span class="ref-cable">counter-balance cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> into hole **18** for counter balance. Add enough weight to lighten the smith carriage.
2. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> into hole **4-R** and <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span> into hole **4-L**. Lift the smith carriage to <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span> <span class="ref-hook">H-1</span>.
3. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">footplate</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S4-R</span> into hole **19-L** and <span class="ref-accessory">footplate</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S4-L</span> into hole **19-R**. For this exercise, the backside of the footplates will be used for support.
4. Unlatch the lever of the leg extension/curl arm and rotate the arm so that it is in approximately the 10 o'clock position.
5. Insert the <span class="ref-accessory">preacher curl bar attachment</span> <span class="ref-accessory">B1-2</span> into hole **29** or **30** of the leg extension/curl arm.
6. Link 2 carabiner clips together and attach <span class="ref-accessory">handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S8</span> to <span class="ref-accessory">extension bar</span> <span class="ref-accessory">B1-2</span>.
7. With the backrest in the flat position, slide the bench to position **8** (adjust to preference) and lock into place.

## Back Extension

1. Firmly grab the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S8</span>.
2. Place feet onto the back of the <span class="ref-accessory">footplates</span>.
3. Pull the weight back by extending the body backwards, bending at the waist.
4. Pause briefly in the fully extended position, then return to the starting position.
5. Instructions can also be found on the video CD that was included with your machine.

## Exercise Precautions

- Be sure the smith carriage is all the way up and locked into place.
- Be sure that the attachments are locked into place.

## After Completing

1. Remove <span class="ref-accessory">handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S8</span> from the <span class="ref-accessory">extension bar</span> <span class="ref-accessory">B1-2</span>.
2. Remove <span class="ref-accessory">extension bar</span> <span class="ref-accessory">B1-2</span>.
3. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">footplates</span> and return to the accessory rack.
4. Unlock the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span> and <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> and lower the smith carriage to rest on the springs.
5. Remove weight plates from the counter balance weight post.
6. Remove <span class="ref-cable">cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> from hole **18** and place into storage hole.
