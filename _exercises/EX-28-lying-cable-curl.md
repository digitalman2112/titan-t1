## Setup

1. Move the bench completely forward to position **1** and incline the backrest to the highest position.
2. Attach <span class="ref-cable">lat pulley cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C3</span> to the attachment pin located at the center of the smith carriage crossbar.
3. Using a clip, attach <span class="ref-cable">extension cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C4</span> to the end of <span class="ref-cable">C3</span> hanging overhead at the forefront of the machine.
4. At the base of the machine, attach <span class="ref-cable">attachment cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C5</span> to the other end of <span class="ref-cable">C4</span>.
5. Attach <span class="ref-accessory">curl bar</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S8</span> to <span class="ref-cable">C5</span>.
6. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">small footplate</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S3-L</span> into hole **21-R** and <span class="ref-accessory">S3-R</span> into hole **21-L** in the lat pulley support bar.
7. Add the desired weight and ensure weights are evenly distributed on each side of the machine.
8. If the weight of the smith carriage is too heavy, attach <span class="ref-cable">counter-balance cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> to hole **18** and add the desired counter weight.

## Lying Cable Curl

1. Firmly grasp the <span class="ref-accessory">curl bar</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S8</span> with both hands.
2. Lay on the floor and place feet squarely on the <span class="ref-accessory">footplates</span>. Keep knees slightly bent (flexed).
3. In a slow and controlled manner, lift weight by curling the bar toward the upper chest.
4. Pause briefly in the fully contracted position and return to the starting position.

## After Completing

1. If the counter-balance was used, remove any weight from the weight post, then remove <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> from hole **18** and return to the storage hole.
2. Unclip and remove <span class="ref-cable">C4</span> from <span class="ref-cable">C3</span>.
3. Remove <span class="ref-cable">C3</span> and attach to the "Storage for C-3" pin.
4. Remove <span class="ref-accessory">footplates</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S3-L</span> and <span class="ref-accessory">S3-R</span>.
