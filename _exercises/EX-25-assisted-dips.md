## Setup

1. Attach <span class="ref-cable">counter-balance cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> into hole **18** on the smith machine carriage bar.
2. Carefully begin loading weight plates onto the counter-balance weight posts. When the plates exceed the weight of the smith machine (approximately 80 lbs. plus attachments), the smith machine will begin to rise. Slowly allow it to reach its highest point, then continue adding the desired counter weight.
3. Attach <span class="ref-accessory">dip bar handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S6-R</span> into hole **10-L** or **11-L**, and handle <span class="ref-accessory">S6-L</span> into hole **10-R** or **11-R** according to preference.
4. Adjust the bench to position **15** (adjust to preference) and lock into place.

## Assisted Dips

1. With one knee resting on the bench, firmly grab the <span class="ref-accessory">dip bar handles</span>.
2. Lift the weight by extending the arms downward and then returning to the starting position in a smooth and controlled manner.
3. Upon completion, lower the weight gently by slowly lifting the arms to the original starting position.

## After Completing

1. Remove <span class="ref-accessory">handles</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S6-R</span> and <span class="ref-accessory">S6-L</span>.
2. Carefully remove the weight from the counter-balance weight post.
3. Remove <span class="ref-cable">cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> from hole **18** and return to storage for C2.

## Precautions

- Be sure that weights are evenly distributed on each side of the smith carriage.
- Check that all cables and attachments are properly connected.
- After the bench is properly positioned, ensure that it is locked into place.
