## Setup

1. Attach the <span class="ref-accessory">safety spotters</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S2</span> to hooks <span class="ref-hook">H-5</span>.
2. Adjust the bench to position **7** (adjust to preference) and ensure that it is locked into place.
3. Adjust the backrest to the desired inclined position using the pull pin located behind the back of the seat. Adjust the seat height to the desired position using the pull pin located underneath the seat.
4. Place the olympic bar onto hooks <span class="ref-hook">H-3</span> or <span class="ref-hook">H-4</span>, depending on the height of the inclined bench.
5. Ensure that the bar is centered on the apparatus and evenly distribute the desired weights on each side of the bar.
6. Sit on the bench and assume a comfortable reclining position with your back resting firmly against the back of the seat and your feet on the provided footrest.

## Incline Chest Press

1. Prior to unracking the bar, be sure to securely grip the bar with hands properly spaced equidistant from the center of the bar.
2. In a slow and controlled manner, lift weight by bar, pause briefly in the near fully extended position, and return to the starting position.
3. When finished, place the olympic bar back onto the original hooks.

## After Completing

1. Re-rack the olympic bar securely onto the original hooks.
2. Remove all weight from the bar.
3. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">safety spotters</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S2</span> from the hooks.

## Precautions

- Exercise extreme caution when loading and unloading the weight plates.
- Be sure that the weights are evenly distributed on each side of the bar and that the collars are securely fastened at the ends of the plate rungs.
- After the bench is properly positioned, ensure that it is locked into place.
- Make sure that both <span class="ref-accessory">safety spotters</span> are set at the proper height to limit the lowest desired safe range of travel of the olympic bar and weights.
