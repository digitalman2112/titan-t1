## Setup

1. Adjust the bench to position **16** (adjust to preference) and lock it into place.
2. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> into hole **7-R** and handle <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span> into hole **7-L**.
3. Lift the smith carriage and lock both <span class="ref-accessory">handles</span> onto hooks <span class="ref-hook">H-5</span>.
4. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">footplate</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S3-L</span> into hole **16-L** and footplate <span class="ref-accessory">S3-R</span> into hole **16-R**. Ensure both are fully and completely inserted — these footplates do not have a locking device and can slide out.
5. Add the desired weight, distributed evenly on both sides. Fasten collars securely at the ends of the plate rungs.

## Leg Press

1. Sit against the bench with feet flat against the <span class="ref-accessory">footplates</span>.
2. Press slightly against the <span class="ref-accessory">footplates</span> and unlock both <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> simultaneously.
3. In a slow, controlled manner, extend your legs upward to a near fully extended position — **do not fully lock your knees.**
4. Pause briefly and return slowly to the starting position.
5. When finished, lock both <span class="ref-accessory">handles</span> back onto the <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span>.

## After Completing

1. Carefully remove all weight from the weight posts.
2. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">footplates</span> and store them on the machine's storage rack.
3. From the standing position, unlock the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> from the <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span> and lower the smith carriage to rest on the springs at the base of the machine.

## Precautions

- The <span class="ref-accessory">footplates</span> do not have a locking device — ensure they are **completely** inserted into the attachment slots before loading weight. They can slide out and cause injury.
- Always ensure **both** <span class="ref-accessory">handles</span> are securely locked before starting and when finishing. Never secure the smith carriage by locking only one side.
- After adjusting the bench position, confirm it is locked into place before use.
- Do not extend your knees to the fully locked position.
