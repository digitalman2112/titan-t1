## Setup

1. Attach <span class="ref-cable">counter-balance cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> into hole **18** on the smith machine carriage bar.
2. Carefully begin loading weight plates onto the weight posts of the counter-balance system. When the weight plates exceed the weight of the smith machine (approximately 80 lbs. plus the weight of exercise handles and attachments), the smith machine will begin to rise. Slowly allow it to reach its highest point, then continue adding the desired counter weight.
3. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span> into hole **2-L** or **3-L**, and handle <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> into hole **2-R** or **3-R** on the smith carriage, at a height where the arms are fully extended from the seated position.
4. Adjust the bench to position **25** and lock into place.
5. Raise the thigh roller pads to the vertical position and adjust the seat up if necessary to keep the knees firmly against the pad. Be sure that the thigh pad pull pin is fully engaged and locked in position.
6. Sit upright on the seat with thighs securely positioned under the thigh roller pads. Place feet squarely on the floor approximately shoulder-width apart.

## Lat Pull Down

1. In a slow, controlled manner, pull the <span class="ref-accessory">handles</span> down toward the upper chest.
2. Pause briefly in the fully contracted position and then return slowly to the starting position.

## After Completing

1. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> and <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span> from the smith carriage.
2. Slowly remove the weights from the counter-balance weight posts.
3. Remove <span class="ref-cable">cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> from hole **18** and return it to its storage hole.

## Precautions

- Check that all cables and attachments are properly connected.
- When loading and unloading weight to the counter-balance system, be careful as the balance shifts between the two sides.
- After the bench is properly positioned, ensure that it is locked into place.
