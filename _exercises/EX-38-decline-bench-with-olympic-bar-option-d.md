## Setup

1. Attach <span class="ref-cable">counter-balance cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> into hole **18** for counter balance. Add enough weight to lighten the smith carriage.
2. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> into hole **2-R** and <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span> into hole **2-L**. Lift the smith carriage to <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span> <span class="ref-hook">H-1</span>.
3. Adjust the backrest and seat to the desired position using the pull pin located underneath. After the bench is properly positioned, ensure that it is locked into place.
4. Insert the <span class="ref-accessory">decline attachment</span> <span class="ref-accessory">D-1</span> into hole **26** located on the top of the backrest. Be sure that the decline attachment is inserted securely.
5. Place the Olympic bar on <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span> <span class="ref-hook">H-4</span> or <span class="ref-hook">H-5</span>.
6. Attach the <span class="ref-accessory">safety spotters</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S2</span> to <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span> <span class="ref-hook">H-6</span> or <span class="ref-hook">H-7</span>. **Note:** The <span class="ref-accessory">safety spotters</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S2</span> can be placed between the two hooks by centering the safety spotter on the hook and locking into place.

## Decline Bench with Olympic Bar

1. Enter the machine carefully and lay down in the declined position.
2. In a slow and controlled manner, lift weight by the bar, pause briefly in the near fully extended position, and return to the starting position.
3. Exit the machine carefully.
4. Instructions can also be found on the video CD that was included with your machine.

## Exercise Precautions

- Make sure that both safety bars are set at the proper height to limit the lowest desired safe range of travel of the Olympic bar and weights.
- Be sure that the weights are evenly distributed on each side of the bar and that the collars are securely fastened at the ends of the plate rungs.
- Enter and exit the machine carefully.

## After Completing

1. Remove the plates from the Olympic bar and then remove the Olympic bar from the <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span>.
2. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">safety spotters</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S2</span>.
3. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">decline attachment</span> <span class="ref-accessory">D-1</span> from hole **26**.
4. Unlock the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> and lower the smith carriage to rest on the bottom springs.
5. Remove the weight plates from the counter balance weight posts.
6. Remove <span class="ref-cable">cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> from hole **18** and place into the storage hole for C-2.
