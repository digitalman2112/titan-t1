## Setup

1. Hook <span class="ref-cable">lat pulley cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C3</span> onto the locking pin located at the center of the smith carriage crossbar. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> from the smith carriage if necessary, and any other handles or attachments.
2. Using a clip, attach <span class="ref-accessory">lat pull down bar</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S9</span> to <span class="ref-cable">C3</span> hanging overhead at the forefront of the machine. If the bar is too high to reach, use <span class="ref-cable">attachment cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C5</span> or an additional carabiner clip to lengthen the cable.
3. Add the desired weight and be sure the weights are evenly distributed on each side of the machine.
4. If the weight of the smith carriage without added weight is too heavy, insert <span class="ref-cable">counter-balance cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> into hole **18** and add the desired counter weight.
5. Raise the thigh roller pads to the upright position. Be sure that the pull pin is fully engaged and locked in position.
6. Adjust the bench to position **16** (adjust to preference) and lock into place.
7. Stand straddling the seat and firmly grasp the <span class="ref-accessory">lat bar</span> handles.
8. Sit on the seat with thighs securely positioned under the thigh roller pads. If the thighs do not rest firmly under the pads, raise the seat by one or two notches.
9. Place feet squarely on the floor approximately shoulder-width apart.

## Lat Pull Down

1. In a slow and controlled manner, pull the <span class="ref-accessory">lat bar</span> down to the upper chest.
2. Pause briefly in the fully contracted position and return slowly to the starting position.

## After Completing

1. If a counter-balance was used, remove any weight from the weight post, then remove <span class="ref-cable">cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> from hole **18** and return it to its storage hole.
2. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">lat pull down bar</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S9</span>.
3. Unclip and remove <span class="ref-cable">C3</span> from the smith carriage crossbar and attach to the storage pin for C3.
