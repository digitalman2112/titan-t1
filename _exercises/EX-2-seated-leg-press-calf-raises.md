## Setup

1. Unlock the bench so that it moves forward and back freely.
2. With the backrest flat, raise the seat to an inclined position — the seat will serve as the backrest for this exercise.
3. Insert <span class="ref-cable">leg press cables</span> <span class="ref-cable">C1-L</span> and <span class="ref-cable">C1-R</span> into holes **13-L** and **13-R**, respectively.
4. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">locking exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span> into hole **3-L** and handle <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> into hole **3-R**.
5. Lift the smith carriage and lock both handles onto hooks <span class="ref-hook">H-3</span> or <span class="ref-hook">H-4</span>.
6. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">footplate</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S4-L</span> into attachment hole **20-L** and footplate <span class="ref-accessory">S4-R</span> into hole **20-R**.
   - For lower leg presses, insert footplates into holes **19-L and 19-R** instead.
   - Lower holes (19) emphasize the upper quadriceps; upper holes (20) emphasize the lower quadriceps.
7. Add the desired weight, distributed evenly on both sides. Fasten collars securely at the ends of the plate rungs.

## Leg Press

1. Sit against the inclined seat with feet flat against the <span class="ref-accessory">footplates</span>.
2. In a slow and controlled manner, unlock both <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> simultaneously.
3. Extend your legs by pressing against the <span class="ref-accessory">footplates</span>.
4. Pause briefly in the near fully extended position — **do not fully lock your knees.**
5. Return slowly to the starting position.
6. When finished, lock both <span class="ref-accessory">handles</span> back onto the <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span>.

## Calf Raises

1. From the leg press starting position, shift your feet so only the **ball of the foot and toes** rest on the **bottom edge** of the <span class="ref-accessory">footplates</span>.
2. Unlock both <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span>.
3. Press up and down through the ankles using the calf muscles.
4. When finished, lock both <span class="ref-accessory">handles</span> back onto the <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span>.

## After Completing

1. Carefully remove all weight from the weight posts.
2. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">footplates</span> and store them on the machine's storage rack.
3. Remove <span class="ref-cable">leg press cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C1-L</span> and store it in hole **23-L**.
4. Remove <span class="ref-cable">leg press cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C1-R</span> and store it in hole **23-R**.
5. Unlock the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> from the <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span> and lower the smith carriage so it rests on the springs.

## Precautions

- Always ensure **both** <span class="ref-accessory">handles</span> of the smith machine are securely locked before starting and when finishing. Never secure the smith carriage by locking only one side.
- Do not extend your knees to the fully locked position during either movement.
- Confirm that <span class="ref-accessory">foot plates</span>, <span class="ref-accessory">handles</span>, and <span class="ref-cable">cables</span> are securely attached before loading weight.
- Exit the machine carefully.
