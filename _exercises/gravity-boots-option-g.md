## Setup

1. At the back of the machine, place the Olympic bar onto the top <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span> (by holes **25**).
2. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">S10-R</span> into the upper hole of **25-L** and <span class="ref-accessory">S10-L</span> into **25-R** so that the handle comes across the top of the Olympic bar and locks it into the <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span>.
3. Attach the <span class="ref-accessory">gravity boots</span> (not included) to each ankle.
4. Attach <span class="ref-accessory">handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S6-R</span> to **14-L** and <span class="ref-accessory">S6-L</span> to **14-R**.
5. Adjust the bench forward to position **1**.

## Gravity Boots

1. Grab the Olympic bar with both hands, and with one foot supported by the smith carriage crossbar, lift the opposite leg up to the Olympic bar and attach the gravity boot. Then lift the other leg up and attach the gravity boot to the Olympic bar.
2. Carefully release your grip on the Olympic bar and begin the exercise.
3. Use the <span class="ref-accessory">handles</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S6</span> to help lower or raise the body.
4. Instructions can also be found on the video CD that was included with your machine.

## Precautions

- Be sure the Olympic bar and locking handles are securely locked into the hooks before use.
- Be sure the gravity boots are securely fastened to each ankle before releasing your grip.
