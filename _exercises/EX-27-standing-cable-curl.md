## Setup

1. Move the bench completely forward to position **1** and incline the backrest to the highest position.
2. Attach <span class="ref-cable">lat pulley cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C3</span> to the attachment pin located at the center of the smith carriage crossbar.
3. Using a clip, attach <span class="ref-cable">extension cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C4</span> to the end of <span class="ref-cable">C3</span> hanging overhead at the forefront of the machine.
4. At the base of the machine, attach <span class="ref-cable">attachment cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C5</span> to the other end of <span class="ref-cable">C4</span>.
5. Attach <span class="ref-accessory">curl bar</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S8</span> to <span class="ref-cable">C5</span>.
6. Add the desired weight and ensure weights are evenly distributed on each side of the machine.
7. If the weight of the smith carriage is too heavy, attach <span class="ref-cable">counter-balance cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> to hole **18** and add the desired counter weight.

## Standing Cable Curl

1. Stand facing the machine just behind the lower frame brace and firmly grasp the <span class="ref-accessory">curl bar</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S8</span>.
2. In a slow and controlled manner, lift weight by curling the bar toward the upper chest.
3. Pause briefly in the fully contracted position and then return to the starting position.
4. Do not lean forward or backward or use the lower back.

## After Completing

1. Unclip and remove <span class="ref-cable">C4</span> from <span class="ref-cable">C3</span>.
2. Remove any weight from the counter-balance system or smith carriage.
3. Remove <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> from hole **18** and hook onto the storage pin.
4. Remove <span class="ref-cable">C3</span> and attach to the "Storage for C-3" pin.

## Precautions

- Check that the safety clip attaching the handle to the cable is in proper working condition and shows no signs of wear.
- Do not lean forward or backward or use the lower back.
