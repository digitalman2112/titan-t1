## Setup

1. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span> into hole **9-R** and <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> into hole **9-L** on the backside of the smith carriage.
2. Lift the smith carriage and lock the handles onto <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span> <span class="ref-hook">H-13</span>.
3. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">dumbbell spotter</span> <span class="ref-accessory">E1-R</span> into hole **16-R** and <span class="ref-accessory">E1-L</span> into hole **16-L**.
4. Select your dumbbells and place them securely onto the <span class="ref-accessory">dumbbell spotters</span> <span class="ref-accessory">E-1</span>.
5. Unlock the bench and slide it under the <span class="ref-accessory">dumbbell spotters</span>. Do not lock the bench.

## Dumbbell Press

1. Firmly grasp the dumbbells with the feet planted firmly on the ground and extend the arms upward.
2. Use the legs to pull the bench out from underneath the <span class="ref-accessory">dumbbell spotters</span> and begin exercising.
3. When finished, use your legs to push the bench back and, with the arms extended, guide the dumbbells back into the <span class="ref-accessory">dumbbell spotters</span>.
4. Be sure that the dumbbells are secure on the <span class="ref-accessory">dumbbell spotters</span> before releasing.
5. Instructions can also be found on the video CD that was included with your machine.

## Exercise Precautions

- Be sure the <span class="ref-accessory">dumbbell spotters</span> are locked into the holes before beginning exercise.
- Be sure that the dumbbells are secure on the <span class="ref-accessory">dumbbell spotters</span> before releasing.

## After Completing

1. Remove the dumbbells from the <span class="ref-accessory">dumbbell spotters</span>.
2. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">dumbbell spotters</span> <span class="ref-accessory">E1-R</span> and <span class="ref-accessory">E1-L</span> from the smith carriage.
3. Unlock the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> and lower the smith carriage to the bottom springs.
4. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span>.
