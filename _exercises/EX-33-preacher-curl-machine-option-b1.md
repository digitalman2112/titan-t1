## Setup

1. Attach <span class="ref-cable">counter-balance cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> into hole **18** for counter balance. Add enough weight to lighten the smith carriage.
2. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> into hole **4-R** and <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span> into hole **4-L**. Lift the smith carriage to <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span> <span class="ref-hook">H-1</span>.
3. Attach the <span class="ref-accessory">preacher curl chest pad</span> <span class="ref-accessory">B1-1</span> onto <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span> <span class="ref-hook">H-11</span>. The pad should be facing the front of the machine and hole **34** should be facing upwards.
4. Adjust the rotating arm by unlocking the lever and lock at approximately the 7 o'clock position.
5. Insert the <span class="ref-accessory">preacher curl armrest pad</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S7</span> into hole **34** located on the <span class="ref-accessory">chest pad attachment</span> <span class="ref-accessory">B1-1</span>.
6. Attach the <span class="ref-accessory">preacher curl bar attachment</span> <span class="ref-accessory">B1-2</span> into hole **30** on the arm of the leg extension attachment and lock into place.
7. Link 2 carabiner clips together and attach <span class="ref-accessory">handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S8</span> to <span class="ref-accessory">extension bar</span> <span class="ref-accessory">B1-2</span>.
8. Incline the bench to approximately 20°, unlock and slide it forward to position **20** (adjust to preference), and then lock into place.
9. Extend the arms over the <span class="ref-accessory">preacher pad</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S7</span>, grasp the <span class="ref-accessory">handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S8</span> firmly, and sit on the bench to begin exercise.

## Preacher Curl Machine

1. Extend arms over the <span class="ref-accessory">preacher pad</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S7</span> and firmly grasp the <span class="ref-accessory">handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S8</span>.
2. In a slow, controlled manner, curl the preacher curl bar up towards the upper chest.
3. Pause briefly in the fully contracted position and return to the starting position.
4. Instructions can also be found on the video CD that was included with your machine.

## Prior to Using This Exercise

- Be sure the smith carriage is all the way up and locked into place.
- Be sure that the attachments are locked into place.

## After Completing

1. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">preacher curl bar attachment</span> <span class="ref-accessory">B1-2</span> from hole **30** and detach <span class="ref-accessory">handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S8</span>.
2. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">preacher curl armrest pad</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S7</span> from the <span class="ref-accessory">preacher curl chest pad</span> <span class="ref-accessory">B1-1</span>.
3. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">preacher curl chest pad</span> <span class="ref-accessory">B1-1</span>.
4. Remove plates from the counter balance.
5. Remove <span class="ref-cable">cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> from hole **18** and insert into storage hole for C-2.
6. Unlock <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S-1</span> and lower the smith carriage onto the springs.

## Precautions

- Be sure the smith carriage is all the way up and locked into place before use.
- Be sure that all attachments are locked into place before use.
