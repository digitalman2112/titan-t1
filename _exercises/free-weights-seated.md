## Setup

1. Attach the <span class="ref-accessory">safety spotters</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S2</span> onto the hooks at the desired height. Make sure that both safety bars are set at the proper height to limit the lowest desired safe range of travel of the olympic bar and weights.
2. Place the olympic bar onto the proper hooks to begin your exercise.
3. Adjust the bench to the proper position and ensure that it is locked into place.
4. Ensure that the bar is centered on the apparatus and evenly distribute the desired weights on each side of the bar.
5. Prior to unracking the bar, be sure to securely grip the bar with hands properly spaced equidistant from the center of the bar.
6. Maintain a stable base of support with your feet planted securely on the floor when performing the exercise.

## Exercise

1. In a slow and controlled manner, perform the exercise.
2. Pause briefly at the end of the range of motion and return to the starting position.
3. When racking the bar, make sure that the bar is securely retained by the barbell supports before releasing your pressure and grip on the bar.

## After Completing

1. Re-rack the olympic bar securely onto the original hooks.
2. Remove all weight from the bar.
3. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">safety spotters</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S2</span> from the hooks and store them.

## Precautions

- Make sure that both <span class="ref-accessory">safety spotters</span> are set at the proper height to limit the lowest desired safe range of travel of the olympic bar and weights.
- Ensure that the bar is centered on the apparatus and that weights are evenly distributed on each side of the bar. 
- Prior to unracking the bar, be sure to securely grip the bar with hands properly spaced equidistant from the center of the bar.
- After the bench is properly positioned, ensure that it is locked into place.
- Perform the exercise in a slow and controlled motion.
