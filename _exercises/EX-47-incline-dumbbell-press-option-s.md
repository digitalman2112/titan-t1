## Setup

1. Place the Olympic bar onto <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span> <span class="ref-hook">H-2</span>.
2. Attach the <span class="ref-accessory">Option S</span> hangers to the dumbbells by tightening the knobs so that they are securely fastened to the grip of each dumbbell.
3. Hang the dumbbells in the starting position (shoulder width apart) by hooking the upper hooks of <span class="ref-accessory">Option S</span> onto the Olympic bar.
4. Adjust the bench to position **7** (adjust to preference) and ensure that it is locked into place.
5. Adjust the backrest to the desired inclined position using the pull pin located behind the back of the seat. Adjust the seat height to the desired position using the pull pin located underneath the seat.

## Incline Dumbbell Press

1. Sit on the bench and assume a comfortable reclining position with your back resting firmly against the back of the seat and your feet on the provided footrest.
2. In a slow and controlled manner, lift the dumbbells, pause briefly in the near fully extended position, and return to the starting position.
3. On the final repetition when the arms are extended, place the hooks back onto the Olympic bar. Before releasing the dumbbells, be sure that each hook of the <span class="ref-accessory">Option S</span> is secure.
4. Instructions can also be found on the video CD that was included with your machine.

## Exercise Precautions

- Be sure the knobs are securely tightened before beginning exercise.
- Upon completion of the exercise, be sure that each hook of the <span class="ref-accessory">Option S</span> is secure on the Olympic bar.
