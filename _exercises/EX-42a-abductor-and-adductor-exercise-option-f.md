## Setup

1. Using an attachment clip, attach <span class="ref-cable">cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C8</span> to the attachment bar located on the inside of the counter-balance.
2. Place the Olympic bar on the <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span> at the rear of the machine at about mid-chest height.
3. Attach <span class="ref-accessory">pulley</span> <span class="ref-accessory">F-1</span> to the other end of <span class="ref-cable">cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C8</span>.
4. Attach <span class="ref-cable">cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C9</span> to hole **32** located by the lower pulley.
5. Attach <span class="ref-cable">cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C10</span> to the other end of <span class="ref-cable">cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C9</span>.
6. Attach the <span class="ref-accessory">ankle strap</span> to the end of <span class="ref-cable">cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C10</span>.

## Abductor and Adductor Exercise

1. Attach the <span class="ref-accessory">ankle strap</span> to the ankle and grab the Olympic bar for support.
2. Stand facing the machine and extend the legs in a sideward motion and return to the starting position.
3. Instructions can also be found on the video CD that was included with your machine.

## Precautions

- Be sure that all cables and pulleys are properly and securely attached before use.
