## Setup

1. Remove any <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> or attachments from the smith carriage.
2. Insert <span class="ref-cable">crossover cable</span> A1-L into hole **7-L** on the crossover arm. Insert <span class="ref-cable">A1-R</span> into the corresponding hole on the right side.
3. Using a clip, attach <span class="ref-cable">extension cable</span> C7-L to <span class="ref-cable">cable</span> C9-L and attach <span class="ref-cable">C7-R</span> to <span class="ref-cable">C9-R</span>.
4. Attach <span class="ref-cable">low pulley cable</span> C6-L to the arm of the cable cross over, and attach <span class="ref-cable">C6-R</span> to the other side.
5. Using a clip, attach the D-grip handles to <span class="ref-cable">C7-L</span> and <span class="ref-cable">C7-R</span>.
6. Disconnect <span class="ref-cable">C9-L</span> from storage and <span class="ref-cable">C9-R</span> from storage. Make sure the cables are not twisted.
7. Select the desired weight.
8. If the weight of the smith carriage is too heavy, insert <span class="ref-cable">counter-balance cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> into hole **18** and add the desired counter weight. Do not exceed your known performance capabilities.

## Low Pulley — Option A1

1. Firmly grasp the D-grip handles in each hand.
2. Assume a comfortable seated position with the back resting against the backrest.
3. Slide the bench to position **5** (adjust to preference) and lock into place.
4. Adjust the backrest and seat to the desired incline position and lock into place using the pull pins underneath.
5. Plant the feet firmly on the ground or use the provided footrest.
6. Perform the desired exercise (incline/decline/chest press, cable pec fly, or overhead triceps) by lifting and lowering the weight in a slow, controlled manner, pausing briefly in the fully contracted position.
7. Instructions can also be found on the video CD that was included with your machine.

## Prior to Using This Exercise

- Be sure that the pull pin is fully engaged and locked in position.
- After the bench is properly positioned, ensure that it is locked into place.

## After Completing

1. If the counter-balance was used, remove any weight from the weight post, then remove <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> from hole **18** and return to the storage hole.
2. Disconnect <span class="ref-cable">C6-L</span> and <span class="ref-cable">C7-L</span> and <span class="ref-cable">C6-R</span> and <span class="ref-cable">C7-R</span> to storage hole for C6-L and C6-R and C7-R.
3. Remove pulleys <span class="ref-cable">A1-L</span> and <span class="ref-cable">A1-R</span> and insert into holes **28-L** and **28-R** located on the crossover arms.
4. Wrap the <span class="ref-cable">C7</span> cables around the lower pulley and loop end over the black plastic ball for storage.

## Precautions

- Be sure that all cables and pulleys are properly and securely attached.
