## Setup

1. Slide the bench completely forward to position **1** and incline the backrest to the high position.
2. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handle</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S1-R</span> into hole **10-L** and handle <span class="ref-accessory">S1-L</span> into hole **10-R** on the back side of the smith carriage.
3. Lift the smith carriage and lock both <span class="ref-accessory">handles</span> onto hooks <span class="ref-hook">H-10</span> or <span class="ref-hook">H-11</span>.
4. Insert <span class="ref-accessory">shoulder pad</span> <span class="ref-accessory">S5-L</span> into hole **16-L** and pad <span class="ref-accessory">S5-R</span> into hole **16-R**. Ensure both are fully seated and will not slide out.
5. If the smith carriage is too heavy without added weight, insert <span class="ref-cable">counter-balance cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> into hole **18** and add the desired counter weight.
6. Select the desired weight.

## Squats

1. Stand beneath the <span class="ref-accessory">shoulder pads</span> with feet planted firmly on the floor, shoulder-width apart. Position the pads so they rest firmly on your shoulders.
2. Unlock both <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> simultaneously.
3. In a slow, controlled manner, squat down by bending the knees.
4. Push back up by straightening the knees toward the starting position.
5. When finished, lower the smith carriage gently and lock both <span class="ref-accessory">handles</span> onto the <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span>.

## Calf Raises

1. From the standing position beneath the <span class="ref-accessory">shoulder pads</span>, shift your weight to the balls of your feet.
2. Unlock both <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span>.
3. Press up through the ankles, raising your heels as high as possible.
4. Lower slowly and repeat.
5. When finished, lock both <span class="ref-accessory">handles</span> back onto the <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span>.

## After Completing

1. Carefully remove all weight from the weight posts.
2. Remove the <span class="ref-accessory">shoulder pads</span> and store them on the machine's storage rack.
3. Unlock the <span class="ref-accessory">exercise handles</span> from the <span class="ref-hook">hooks</span> and lower the smith carriage so it rests on the springs.
4. If a counter-balance was used, remove any counter weight from the weight post, then remove <span class="ref-cable">cable</span> <span class="ref-cable">C2</span> from hole **18** and return it to its storage hole.

## Precautions

- Ensure <span class="ref-accessory">shoulder pads</span> are completely inserted into the attachment slots and will not slide out before getting under the carriage.
- Always ensure **both** <span class="ref-accessory">handles</span> are securely locked before starting and when finishing. Never secure the smith carriage by locking only one side.
