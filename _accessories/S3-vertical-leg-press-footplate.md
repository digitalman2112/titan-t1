Footplates insert into attachment holes on the machine frame. Insert S3-L into hole **16-L** and S3-R into hole **16-R** for the standard vertical leg press position. Adjust the bench position to change the angle as needed.

**Caution:** These footplates do not have a locking device. Ensure they are fully and completely inserted into the attachment slots before loading weight â€” they can slide out and cause injury.

**Storage:** Remove footplates and store on the machine's storage rack when not in use.
