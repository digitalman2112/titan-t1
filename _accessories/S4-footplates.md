Footplates insert into attachment holes on the machine frame. The hole position controls which part of the quadriceps is emphasized:

| Hole position | Muscles emphasized |
|---|---|
| **19-L / 19-R** (lower holes) | Upper quadriceps |
| **20-L / 20-R** (upper holes) | Lower quadriceps |

For calf raises, place only the **ball of the foot and toes** on the bottom edge of the footplate rather than the full foot.

**Storage:** Remove footplates and store on the machine's storage rack when not in use.
