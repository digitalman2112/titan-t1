For assisted dips, attach <span class="ref-accessory">S6-R</span> into hole **10-L** or **11-L** and <span class="ref-accessory">S6-L</span> into hole **10-R** or **11-R** according to preference.

For gravity boots, attach <span class="ref-accessory">S6-R</span> to hole **14-L** and <span class="ref-accessory">S6-L</span> to hole **14-R**.

**Storage:** Remove handles and store on the accessory rack when not in use.
