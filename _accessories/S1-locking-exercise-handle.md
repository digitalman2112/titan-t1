The handles insert into holes **3-L** (left) and **3-R** (right) on the machine frame, then hook onto the smith carriage at **hook H-3 or H-4** to hold it in the raised position while you load weight and get into position.

**Important:** Always lock **both** handles before completing or exiting the exercise. Never attempt to secure the smith carriage using only one handle â€” this is a serious safety risk.

To begin the exercise, unlock both handles simultaneously in a slow and controlled manner.
