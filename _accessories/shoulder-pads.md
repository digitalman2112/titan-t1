Shoulder pads insert into the smith carriage crossbar. Insert S5-L into hole **17-L** and S5-R into hole **17-R**. Ensure both pads are fully seated and will not slide out before loading weight or getting under the carriage.

**Storage:** Remove shoulder pads and store on the machine's storage rack when not in use.
