Hook <span class="ref-cable">C11</span> onto the locking pin located at the center of the smith carriage crossbar before releasing the pec deck arms into the exercise position.

**Storage:** After use, remove <span class="ref-cable">C11</span> from the smith carriage crossbar and attach it to the storage pin for C11.
