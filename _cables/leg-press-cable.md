The leg press cables are a matched pair. C1-L mounts on the left side of the machine, C1-R on the right.

**Storage:** Cables store in holes **23-L** and **23-R** when not in use.

**Installation:** Insert C1-L into hole **13-L** and C1-R into hole **13-R** before performing the leg press or calf raise.

Inspect both cable attachments to confirm they are fully seated before adding weight.
