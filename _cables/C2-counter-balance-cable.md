The counter-balance cable is optional. Use it when the weight of the smith carriage without added weight plates is too heavy for the intended exercise.

**Installation:** Insert C2 into hole **18**, then add the desired counter weight to the cable's weight post. Increasing the counter weight reduces the effective load of the carriage.

**Removal:** After completing the exercise, remove any counter weight from the weight post, then remove C2 from hole 18 and return it to its storage hole.
