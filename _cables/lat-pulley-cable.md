Hook C3 onto the locking pin located at the center of the smith carriage crossbar. The cable runs overhead to the forefront of the machine where attachments (lat pull down bar S9, or extension cable C4) are clipped on.

If C3 is too short to reach comfortably, extend it with <span class="ref-cable">attachment cable C5</span> or an additional carabiner clip.

**Storage:** Return C3 to the storage pin on the smith carriage crossbar when not in use.
